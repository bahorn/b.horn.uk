Security

I have found security flaws in:

  • GRUB2, a ton of issues ranging from stack clashes to use-after-frees which resulted in me being able to develop a bypass for UEFI Secure Boot.
  • TP-Link EAP Access Points, a predictable session issue that I exploited via a timing side channel to determine when a user was active.
  • Terramaster NASs, finding a service that wrote your ssh key to `/root/.ssh/authorized_keys`...

The CVEs attributed to me are:
CVE-2024-45781, CVE-2024-45782, CVE-2024-45783, CVE-2025-0622, CVE-2025-0624 and CVE-2025-1118.
